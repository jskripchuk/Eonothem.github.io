About Us

Heya! This calculus problem generator was made by James Skripchuk and Jesse Li. We're both students at The Charter School of Wilmington, and we made this website as our final project for AP Calculus AB. James took care of most of the web-development challenges (because this is also his Web-Dev final), while Jesse solved most of the problem generation tasks by making a super rad problem generator API. Hopefully this website will provide some extra practice for AP students next year. See-ya!

Special thanks goes to Mr. Satalino and Mr. Kramer for being such wonderful human beings.
